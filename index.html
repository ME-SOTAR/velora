Velora
App in development

AI-powered beauty & fashion decisions made easy.

Velora is a mobile app in development that helps users make better beauty and fashion choices using AI-powered tools like makeup matching, outfit selection, hairstyle try-ons, and style analysis.

Based on these tools, Velora recommends relevant beauty and fashion products from partner retailers and brands. Purchases are completed on the retailer’s website via affiliate links.
